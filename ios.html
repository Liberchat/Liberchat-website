Liberchat for iOS

1️⃣ Install AltStore

  1. Visit altstore.io
  2. Download and install AltServer on your Mac or PC
  3. Connect your iPhone via USB
  4. Install AltStore on your iPhone via AltServer

2️⃣ Install Liberchat

  1. On your iPhone, open this page in Safari
  2. Tap the button below
  3. Choose "Open in AltStore"
Install Liberchat

⚠️ Important Notes

  • You must renew the application every 7 days via AltStore
  • Keep AltServer running on your computer
  • Your iPhone and computer must be on the same WiFi network
